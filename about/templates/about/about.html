Our story

Lidya, fashion designer.

As a mother of a healthy child, she recently found more use of her dormant skills in tailoring for repairing clothes, and with inspiration from online DIY culture and positive influences she have become a full fledged practitioner in the arts of sewing, stitching, patterns and seams. However, her experience goes back further than that as tailoring has always been a hobby for Lidya.

Her ambitions may not be grandiose as of yet, but together with good friends and a good website hopefully she will get some attention
